# End-to-End Banking Data Lakehouse on Databricks: Ingestion, Modeling, and Analysis

This project models a Romanian retail banking system. The bank provides a comprehensive range of financial products and services to both individual and corporate customers, including current and savings accounts, debit and credit cards, loans (mortgage, personal, auto, and overdraft), and payment operations across multiple channels (branch, ATM, internet banking, mobile, and POS).

The organization is structured into regionally distributed branches, each staffed with employees specialized in distinct roles such as tellers, advisors, managers, and analysts. All operations are recorded and audited to ensure data integrity and compliance.

Transactional data is processed through an ETL pipeline built on Databricks, enabling scalable data ingestion, transformation, and analytics for reporting and business insights.
